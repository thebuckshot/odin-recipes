Sous-Vide Hollandaise

A delicions and easy recipe to make for Hollandaise!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 TBSP Lemon Juice
  • 2 TBSP Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

    • Steps

    1. Add eggs, lemon juice, water, salt and pepper to a bag
    2. Warm Sous Vide to 145 F
    3. Add bag to sousvide and pin top to side
    4. Let cook for 45 minutes
    5. Add ingredients to a blender
    6. Blend and Serve!
